Asteroid Game

  • C++11 , SFML Framework

The asteroid game is a classic arcade-style game where the player controls a spaceship and navigates through a field of moving asteroids. The goal is to survive by avoiding collisions with asteroids while shooting them down. Destroying larger asteroids spawns smaller ones, adding a layer of challenge to the gameplay.

The key features of the game include :
Smooth spaceship movement with thrust mechanics , Shooting bullets to destroy asteroids , Explosions and animations when objects collide , A dynamic system for generating smaller asteroids when larger ones are destroyed.
